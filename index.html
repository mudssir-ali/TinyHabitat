Our story

Founded in 2022, TiniHabitat is a planetary health company that helps to design tools that help people reconnect with nature and therefore regain a strong sense of self. We are a diverse group of scientists coming together to unearth the secrets behind our complex relationships with nature. Informed by ecology, data science, and social networks, we are developing novel tools to help people not only offset their carbon footprint, but also develop cutting-edge research to help others regain their deepest sense of well-being.

Our mission

We're Dutch historian Rutger Bregman points out that all great societal advances begin as an impossible dream in the mind of someone who most people think is crazy. Written laws, human rights, democracy, suffrage for women, and the abolition of slavery all began this way. What will our new dreams be? One person, one planet, and one future TiniHabitat suggest that we must strive to implement regenerative sustainable practices, one built around common-cause values with justice, fairness, and well-being for all, and one that unites head, heart, and technological advances in pursuit of a healthy whole.
